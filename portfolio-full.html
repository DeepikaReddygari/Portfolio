Thermal Energy Storage System Performance Using PCM-Variable Heat

POCER2019

August 8, 2019

Today the use of PCMs to store heat in the form of latent heat is increasing for solar applications since large quantities of thermal energy are stored in small quantities. In this study, two various phase change materials (PCM) - myristic acid and stearic acid - are used in thermal energy storage (TES). The current system utilizes solar power in the form of sensible heat and latent heat as a heat source to store thermal energy.
